To the Student

I completely understand if you're feeling unsure about tutoring. It might help to think about what you'd like to gain from our sessions—whether that's achieving a specific grade, rebuilding your confidence in mathematics, or simply discovering the enjoyment and deeper meaning behind the formulas and rules you've encountered at school.

Having recently gone through—and continuing to experience—the pressures of exams and the challenges of grasping complex topics myself, I understand how normal it is to struggle without the right support. My aim is to create a relaxed, judgment-free space where you're comfortable sharing your difficulties. Together, we'll tackle complex problems step-by-step, identifying the methods that work best for you. Remember, your educational journey is unique, and I'll be here to support and encourage you every step of the way.

To the Parent

I understand how important it is to find a supportive, effective tutor who genuinely appreciates the educational challenges students face today. Drawing directly from my recent experience with SQA, I am familiar with the frustrations and anxieties both students and parents may encounter.

My tutoring sessions focus on creating a motivating, patient, and adaptable learning environment, enabling your child to confidently tackle challenging material. I aim to provide balanced sessions, ensuring your child thoroughly understands what they've learned in school, while also stretching their mathematical ability to best prepare them for examinations.

I believe every student has the potential to excel in mathematics with the right guidance and encouragement. If you feel my approach aligns with your child's needs, please do reach out—I’d be very happy to arrange an introductory conversation at your convenience.
